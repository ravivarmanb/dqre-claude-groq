UK HEALTH INSURANCE DATA QUALITY POLICY
Version 3.2 | Regulatory Compliance & Data Governance Division
Effective: 2024-01-01

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. MEMBER DATA STANDARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 NHS Number
- All active members MUST have a valid NHS number on file.
- NHS numbers must be exactly 10 digits (no spaces, no dashes).
- NHS number uniqueness must be enforced across all member records.
- Tolerance: 0% missing for active members; 2% for lapsed/cancelled.

1.2 Date of Birth
- Date of birth is mandatory for all members.
- Must be a valid historical date (cannot be in the future).
- Member must be at least 0 days old and no older than 120 years.
- Any date of birth after today's date is a CRITICAL data error.

1.3 Contact Information
- Email address is required for all ACTIVE members (95% completeness target).
- Phone number required for ACTIVE members (90% completeness target).
- Postcode is mandatory for all members; must match UK postcode format.
- UK postcode format: alphanumeric, 6-8 characters, space before last 3 chars.

1.4 Gender
- Gender field must contain only accepted values: M, F, Other.
- NULL gender values are permitted only for records created before 2018.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2. POLICY DATA STANDARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 Premium Amounts
- Monthly premium must be greater than £0.00 for all active policies.
- Premium amounts must not exceed £10,000 per month (flag for review).
- Premium amounts must be rounded to 2 decimal places.

2.2 Policy Dates
- Policy start date is mandatory on all records.
- If end_date is present, it must be strictly after start_date.
- Policies with status LAPSED or CANCELLED must have an end_date.

2.3 Excess (Deductible)
- Excess amount must be non-negative (zero is permitted).
- Excess amount must be populated for COMPREHENSIVE and ELITE policies.
- Standard excess range: £0 to £5,000.

2.4 Policy Type
- Policy type must be one of: BASIC, STANDARD, COMPREHENSIVE, ELITE.
- Each member may hold at most 2 concurrent ACTIVE policies.

2.5 Insurer Code
- Insurer code is mandatory on all policy records.
- Must be a recognised insurer: AXA, BUPA, VITALITY, AVIVA, CIGNA.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3. CLAIMS DATA STANDARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Claim Dates
- Claim submission date must not be earlier than the treatment date.
- Claims must be submitted within 90 days of treatment date.
- Claims submitted more than 90 days after treatment are flagged as LATE.

3.2 Financial Amounts
- Claimed amount must be greater than £0.00.
- Approved amount must not exceed claimed amount.
- Approved amount must be NULL for PENDING/INVESTIGATING claims.

3.3 Provider Validation
- All providers referenced in claims must be registered in the providers table.
- Claims must only reference providers with nhs_registered = 1.
- Inactive providers (active=0) must not appear in new claims.

3.4 Policy Linkage
- Claim must link to a valid policy_id in the policies table.
- Claims on CANCELLED policies are only valid if treatment_date precedes end_date.
- Claims on LAPSED policies require additional review flag.

3.5 Diagnosis Codes
- Diagnosis codes must follow ICD-10 format: one letter followed by digits (e.g. J18.0).
- NULL diagnosis codes are acceptable only for wellness/preventive claims.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4. PREMIUM PAYMENT STANDARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Payment Amounts
- Premium payment amount must match the policy's monthly premium (±£0.01 tolerance).
- Negative payment amounts are NEVER permitted.

4.2 Overdue Detection
- Premium status should be OVERDUE if due_date has passed and paid_date is NULL.
- Overdue premiums older than 90 days require escalation flag.

4.3 Payment Method
- Payment method must be present for PAID status records.
- Accepted methods: DD (Direct Debit), CARD, BACS, CHEQUE.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5. PROVIDER STANDARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 Registration
- All providers must have a valid contract_start date.
- NHS registration status (nhs_registered) must be explicitly set (not NULL).
- Providers without postcodes should be flagged for data enrichment.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. REFERENTIAL INTEGRITY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- Every claim.member_id must exist in members table.
- Every claim.policy_id must exist in policies table.
- Every policy.member_id must exist in members table.
- Every premium.policy_id must exist in policies table.
- Every waitlist.member_id must exist in members table.
- Cross-table member_id on claims must match the member_id on the linked policy.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7. TIMELINESS STANDARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

- Claims in PENDING status for more than 30 days must be flagged.
- Waitlist records with no actual_date and target_date passed must be flagged.
- Overdue premiums (due_date < today, status = PENDING) must be flagged.
- Member registration_date must not be in the future.
