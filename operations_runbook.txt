CHAPTER 5: DATA VALIDATION CHECKPOINTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1 Pre-Processing Validation Gates

Before any claim enters processing workflow:

GATE 1 - Member Verification
□ Member exists and status = ACTIVE
□ Policy is ACTIVE and covers treatment date
□ NHS number on claim matches member record
□ Member age at treatment date is between 0 and 120

GATE 2 - Financial Pre-Check
□ Claimed amount > £0 and < £500,000 (outlier cap)
□ Policy has not exceeded annual benefit limit
□ Excess has been correctly applied to eligible claims

GATE 3 - Provider Check
□ Provider exists in approved network
□ Provider type matches treatment_type category:
  - GP visits → provider_type = 'GP'
  - Surgeries → provider_type = 'HOSPITAL'
  - Consultations → provider_type = 'SPECIALIST' or 'CLINIC'
  - Prescriptions → provider_type = 'PHARMACY'
□ Provider contract is active on treatment date

5.2 Waitlist Data Quality

Waitlist Integrity Rules:
- referral_date must not be in the future
- target_date should be referral_date + standard wait time (varies by treatment)
- If actual_date is populated, status must be COMPLETED
- If status = COMPLETED, actual_date must be populated
- Waitlist records with status WAITING and actual_date populated = data error
- Members with more than 3 concurrent WAITING entries need review

5.3 Premium Reconciliation Rules

Monthly Premium Checks:
- Count of PAID premiums per policy must match months since start_date (for active)
- No policy should have 2 premium records with the same due_date
- DD payment method: paid_date should be within 3 days of due_date
- BACS payment: paid_date should be within 5 days of due_date
- CHEQUE payment: paid_date should be within 10 days of due_date

5.4 Cross-Table Consistency Checks

The following cross-table checks must pass in all environments:
- claims.member_id must equal policies.member_id for the linked policy
- Policy end_date must be after all claim treatment_dates for that policy
- Member registration_date must be on or before policy start_date
- Member registration_date must be on or before all claims for that member
- Providers used in approved claims must have active=1 at time of treatment

CHAPTER 7: QUALITY THRESHOLDS BY SEVERITY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CRITICAL rules (threshold = 100%):
- Negative financial amounts anywhere in system
- Future dates of birth
- Claims on non-existent or non-NHS providers
- Duplicate NHS numbers
- claim_date before treatment_date

HIGH severity rules (threshold = 98%):
- Missing NHS numbers on active members
- Policy excess missing on Comprehensive/Elite
- Rejected claims without rejection_reason
- Claims without diagnosis codes

MEDIUM severity rules (threshold = 95%):
- Missing email on active members
- Missing phone on active members
- Policies without insurer_code

LOW severity rules (threshold = 90%):
- Waitlist target_date missing
- Provider postcode missing

  - All CRITICAL and HIGH severity rules
  - Claims pending > 30 days check
  - Overdue premium detection

Weekly (Monday 07:00 UTC):
  - All MEDIUM severity rules
  - Provider network validation
  - Duplicate detection sweep

Monthly (1st of month):
  - All LOW severity rules
  - Full referential integrity scan
  - Waitlist overdue check
